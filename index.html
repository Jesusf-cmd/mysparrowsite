◆   Roofing FAQ   ◆

Common Roofing Questions

Answers to what Oklahoma homeowners ask us most before calling.

If your roof was damaged by hail, wind, or another covered storm event, your homeowner's insurance policy is typically required to pay for a full replacement — minus your deductible. Sparrow inspects the damage, documents it properly, files the claim on your behalf, and meets the adjuster at your home. In most cases our clients pay only their deductible. We'll be upfront with you about whether your specific damage qualifies before you commit to anything.
No — the inspection is completely free and there is no commitment required after. In insurance claim scenarios, payment typically comes from your insurance company directly. You are responsible for your deductible amount, which is defined in your policy. We never ask for large upfront deposits before work begins, and we'll explain the payment process clearly before any contract is signed.
Most residential roof replacements in Oklahoma take 1–2 days from tear-off to final cleanup. Larger homes or complex rooflines may take an additional day. Commercial roofing timelines vary by scope. We don't leave tarps on your roof for days — we schedule to complete the job in one efficient run.
Yes — this is one of the most important things we do. Insurance adjusters sometimes miss damage or undervalue repairs. We meet the adjuster on-site, walk the roof together, and make sure all legitimate damage is documented and included in the claim.
We're GAF System Certified and CertainTeed ShingleMaster certified — which means we install asphalt shingles, metal roofing, slate, tile, and wood shake for residential properties. For commercial buildings we handle TPO, EPDM, flat roofing, and metal systems.
We're based in Yukon and primarily serve the OKC metro — Oklahoma City, Yukon, Edmond, Moore, Norman, Midwest City, Mustang, Bethany, and Del City. We also serve Tulsa, Enid, Lawton, Ardmore, and other Oklahoma communities. Just call us at (405) 412-2924.
