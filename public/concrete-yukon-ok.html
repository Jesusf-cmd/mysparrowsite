Common Questions

Most Yukon driveways cost between $6–$12 per square foot installed, depending on size, thickness, and finish. A typical 2-car driveway runs $3,500–$7,000.
Yukon sits on heavy clay soil in Canadian County. Without a proper 4–6 inch compacted gravel base, concrete will settle and crack as the clay expands and contracts with moisture.
Yes. We offer stamped, brushed, and smooth finishes for Yukon patios. Stamped concrete can mimic stone, brick, or slate at a fraction of the cost.
Including tear-out, base prep, and pour, most Yukon driveways take 3–5 days. The concrete itself cures for 7 days before vehicle use.
Driveway replacements and larger projects typically require a City of Yukon permit. We handle all permitting as part of the project.
Partial replacement is possible but we often recommend full replacement for the best long-term results, especially if the existing concrete is showing widespread cracking.
