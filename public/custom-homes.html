Our Build Process

From First Call to
Final Walkthrough.

Here's exactly how a Sparrow custom home build works — from your first conversation through the day you get your keys.

01
Consultation & Goals

We sit down and talk through what you want to build, your budget, your timeline, and your land situation. We ask hard questions early so there are no surprises later.

02
Plans & Budgeting

We work from your plans or connect you with a designer. We price the build in detail — material by material, trade by trade — so you know what you're committing to.

03
Pre-Construction & Permitting

We handle all permit applications, soil testing if required, and coordinate with your lender's draw schedule before a shovel hits the ground.

04
Site Prep & Foundation

Land clearing, grading, utility connections, and foundation pour — everything is staged, inspected, and done to spec before framing begins.

05
Framing Through Finishes

Framing, roofing, windows, mechanicals, insulation, drywall, cabinets, flooring, paint, fixtures — we manage every trade through every phase with regular client updates.

06
Final Walkthrough & Keys

We do a detailed walk with you before closing out. Every punch-list item is addressed. You get your warranty documents and your new home. Done right, start to finish.
