Built for Oklahoma

Homes Built for
Oklahoma Weather

Oklahoma's clay soil, severe storms, and temperature extremes demand homes engineered for durability — not just designed for aesthetics.

Engineered for Clay Soil

Post-tension slab foundations and proper drainage systems designed specifically for Oklahoma's expansive clay soil.

Storm-Resistant Construction

Impact-resistant roofing, reinforced framing, and safe room options built to handle Oklahoma's severe weather corridor.

Energy-Efficient Systems

Spray foam insulation, high-efficiency HVAC, and Low-E windows designed for Oklahoma's extreme summer heat and winter cold.

Proper Moisture Management

House wrap, flashing details, and drainage planes that prevent moisture intrusion — critical in Oklahoma's humid climate.
